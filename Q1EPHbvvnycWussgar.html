Exercises for burning calories: the ability to eat everything in a row

Exercises for burning calories: the ability to eat everything in a row

Many people think so, let's see if it is possible to use such a scheme or even practice it on a permanent basis. Let's watch how the Champions of fitness, bodybuilding who often train and have a fast exchange eat substances'.

A person with muscle mass, working hard in the gym and even walking a lot burns a ton of calories! Why not get at least 1,000 calories a day from "junk food" or at least from healthy fruits and nuts?

But, no! Children choke on more "boring" buckwheat and rice, breast and egg whites. Junk food is not allowed every day, and sometimes even once or twice a month. The more famous an athlete is, the stricter his diet is, even in the off-season, when up to "another six months or more.

Why can't you eat sugar, honey, condensed milk and rolls, and therefore work it all out in training?

Useful and empty calories

The food we eat is digested in various ways. Calories derived from complex carbohydrates, lean protein and healthy fats fat serves as a building material for muscles, helps to restore and improve various organs.

Calories obtained from sugar or fruit our body can only use in limited quantities, everything else is either converted into fat, or leaves the body as waste.

It turns out that by adding a lot of empty calories to the diet, you fill your digestive system with useless energy, which is of no energy value, and we create unnecessary problems for ourselves.

In a sense, empty calories are beginning to compete in our diet with healthy calories. The more useless food is consumed, the less our body receives useful products and running does not cancel this fact.

Limited capacity of the digestive system

The idea of competition is not for you everyone understands, so you can remember the example of a person who can digest one glass of milk, but if he drinks three glasses, then he is guaranteed to get diarrhea.

The same goes for protein and other foods. Enzymes in the gut are sufficient for a certain amount of this or that product, then there is a failure, gases are formed, problems with digestion occur.

With such problems faced every person who tried to eat a lot and build large muscles. Each of the products that we consume puts a load on those or others other body. A lot of sugar is a blow to the pancreas, the threat of diabetes, a lot of protein is a load on the kidneys and liver.

Therefore, by adding a lot of empty calories, we not only run the risk of not digesting them or digesting them in the form of fat, we reduce the absorption of useful foods eaten at the same time day.

Excessive increase in training volume is the grave for your results

Let's say we trained in the gym, increasing working weights. This had a good effect on muscle growth, and in dieting conditions on their preservation. To improve or speed up the fat burning process, we add 90 minutes of cardio daily!

The weight on the scales began to decrease, but then "Bang" - the appetite increases sharply! I want to eat more and "breakdowns on the diet" begin. That is, the idea of moving more and burning fat better failed because the body is" too smart".

OK, let's say we have an iron willpower and the diet is not broken, but: cardio work leads not only to an increase in the oxidation of fatty acids (fat burning), but also to an increase in catabolism of all cells, including muscle ones!

Maybe you need to do more weight training? This is better, but still if the volume of loads becomes too large, catabolic processes can take over anabolic and we will burn more muscle mass than is acceptable.

The result will be bad, the weight on the scales will be lost so much not due to fat, but due to the loss of muscle. It turns out that an increase in the volume of training is also necessary increased competition between useful and undesirable workloads, exercise load and recovery after training.

The body's hormonal system won't thank you

Increasing training volume and reducing the proportion of useful foods in the diet in favor of useless ones can lead to consistently high levels of cortisol in the blood. Cortisol can destroy muscle and cause fat deposition in atypical places, and prevent fat burning in General.

Eating sugar and simple carbohydrates will boost vitamin production. A high vitamin is sleepiness and obesity, blocking the processes of fat burning (although plus muscle growth, however, along with fat). Reducing the content of complex carbohydrates in food and increasing the amount of fructose (fruits and dried fruits), on the contrary, reduces the beneficial properties of the vitamin: its participation in the storage of glycogen in the muscles.

This will hit glycogen recovery after training - less energy during exercise time, despite the abundance of empty calories.

In fact, the topic of hormones can be discussed for a long time, but this is not necessary. Any competent endocrinologist will confirm that on healthy food we will have more muscle and less fat, with the same caloric content of the diet.

Conclusions: no one can not give up all delicious, desirable but forbidden products forever. However, in order to get a very good result, it is better to arrange a festive lunch (maximum of 1 time a week), than to worsen your result on a daily basis!

For beginners, it is better to transfer yourself to the correct level for a month, healthy food "to enter the mode" not to think of "Camilo" prematurely.
