2. How to lose weight by 14 kg in 14 days-destroy myths

Breaking down stereotypes is simple: do the opposite, and get exactly the opposite result. These will be proof of the falsity of the cliche about losing weight.

Experiment on yourself is not worth it - why risk your health? This is the work of Methodists. So I, as a Methodist, decided to take a chance and reveal the truth.

The task was to burn 14 kg in 14 days by doing caliperometry and densitometry before and after.

The caliper test and the anthropometric test are available to everyone! You can see that the layer of subcutaneous fat gone, meaning gone is the fat, not the water. The densitometer is a stationary device. It scans your body and shows exactly what is gone: fat, muscle, or water.

I started training daily, eating 200 calories a day! Every day I weighed myself and shot a video, did everything to show that the experiment truthful.

After 14 days, I lost 14 kg of weight! If you believe the myths, I should have lost a maximum of 2 kg of fat. The remaining 12 kg had to be used for water, intestinal contents, and muscles. In this case-instead of relief, I would get "skinny flab". But, even visually, my body became raised, my muscles became rigid and without a drop fat.

Densitometry showed 8% body fat, anthropometric test less than 5% subcutaneous fat. 8% fat in the body in the body, approximately = 4-5% of subcutaneous fat, because in addition to subcutaneous fat, we also have "internal fat".

"Strength" in training also did not decrease, and this indicates that the muscle mass did not suffer, even by 200 cal, per day.

The achieved shape and weight I maintained for a year after losing weight. I increased the mass only when I needed it for competitions (I needed to change my weight class).

Conclusion - in 2 weeks it is quite possible to lose weight by 14 kg, reducing your body weight by about 12%! During this period, it is quite possible to burn 5-7 kilograms of fat, and not 2 kilograms of fat as claimed!
